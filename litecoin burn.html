litecoin (LTC)

Litecoin (LTC) is a cryptocurrency that was designed to provide fast, secure and low-cost payments by taking advantage of the unique properties of blockchain technology. The cryptocurrency was created based on the Bitcoin (BTC) protocol, but it differs in terms of the hashing algorithm used, hard caps, block transaction times and a few other factors. Litecoin's block time is just 2.5 minutes and transaction fees are extremely low, making it suitable for micro transactions and point of sale payments.
