Ethereum burn

Ethereum (ETH)

Crypto burning is the act of sending tokens of crypto coins to a wallet that has no access key, so these tokens are lost forever. The practice is to limit the coins in circulation. In the case of Ethereum ETH, a portion of each transaction fee measured in Ethereum will be burned, following the 2021 EIP-1559 protocol. This will limit the supply and help to increase in price.
