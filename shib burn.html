Shib Inu (SHIB)

 Across the three Shib INU burn wallets, 410.3 trillion Shib INUs have been burned, which is 41% of the total Shib INU supply. However, most of the destroyed Shib INU comes from Vitalik Buterin's initial burning. Ethereum's creators liquidated 410.24 Trillion ShiB INU in May 2021, receiving 500 Trillion ShiB INU when the token was first launched, 50% of the total supply was. Buterin then donated the rest of the Shib to INU, which is used by the India Crypto COVID-19 Relief Fund. This means that after subtracting Buterin's burns from the total amount of Shiba Inu, the community has burned 59.2 billion Shiba Inu, worth $1.63m.
