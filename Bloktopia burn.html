bloktopia (BLOK)

Bloktopia (BLOK) is a decentralized metaverse supported and built by Polygon. In bloktopia, a decentralized virtual reality skyscraper composed of 21 levels (21 million bitcoins as recognized) serves as a central hub for users to access crypto information and immersive content in one place. So-called bloktopia can educate themselves in learning the basics or more advanced crypto, earn revenue by owning virtual real estate, playing games, building networks and much more. In addition, advertisers and sponsors can access the platform's users through a dedicated NFT mechanism. Using a VR experience, users can engage in an immersive environment in accordance with Bloktopia's four main pillars Play, Learn, Earn and Create.
