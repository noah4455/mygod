Polygon (MATIC)

Polygon MATIC is the first properly based smooth to use platform for the Ethereum scaling and for the infrastructure improvement. Its core component is the Polygon SDK, a modular, flexible framework that supports building a wide variety of applications. Polygon turns Ethereum into a complete multichain system. This multichain system is similar to other systems such as Avalanche, Cosmos, Polka-dot etc. with the benefits of Ethereum security vibrant ecosystem.
