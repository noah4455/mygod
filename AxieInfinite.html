axie Infinity(AXS)

Axie Infinity(AXS) is a blockchain based on trading and fighting game, which is partially operated and owned by its players. Inspired by popular games like Pokémon and Tamagotchi, Axie Infinity allows players to collect, breed, raise, battle, and trade token-based creatures known as Axis. Axie Infinity has its unique ecosystem known as AXS (Axie Infinity Shards). These are used to participate in key governance votes and will allow holders to specify how money is spent in the Axie Community Fund.
