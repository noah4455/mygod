cosmos (ATOM)

The cosmos is a cryptocurrency that is designed to power the blockchain environment. The team's goal is to build an internet of blockchains, A network of blockchains that can communicate with each other in a decentralized manner. Cosmos is a proof-of-stake chain. ATOM holders can stake their tokens to maintain the network and receive more ATOMs as rewards.
