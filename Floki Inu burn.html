Floki Inu (FLOKI)

Floki Inu team announced that Floki Inu exists in two chains. One is Ethereum and the other is Binance Smart Chain. When Floki Inu launched, a maximum supply of 10 trillion on Ethereum Chain and on the Binance Smart Chain with the same supply. As such, Floki Inu has a total maximum supply of 20 trillion, but the team noted that 10.65 trillion tokens on both chains were permanently burned, which Coinmarketcap(CMC) may have been unaware of.
