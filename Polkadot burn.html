Polkadot (DOT)

Polka-dot is an open-source shard multichain protocol that connects and secures networks of specialized blockchains, facilitating cross-chain transfers of any data or asset type, not just tokens, allowing blockchains to interact with each other. Allows to be interoperable with. Polka-dot was designed to provide a foundation for the decentralized blockchain Internet, also known as Web 3. Polka-dot is known as the layer-0 metaprotocol Describes a format for a network of blockchains known as para chains (parallel chains). As a metaprotocol, Polka-dot is able to autonomously and seamlessly update its own codebase through on-chain governance as per the wishes of its token holder community.
